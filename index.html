Welcome to Paws 'n Hand

Since 2009, Paws 'n Hand has been providing exceptional pet sitting and dog walking services in Oakland and Piedmont, California.

Paws 'n Hand is best known for it's happy and satisfied customers -- pets and their parents alike. We offer thorough, flexible and dependable care. Clients enjoy the peace of mind that is provided through excellent communication, tailored to the preferences of each pet parent. Paws 'n Hand partners with the pet parents to create the best possible experience for their pet family.

We offer pet care services for cats, dogs, bunnies, birds, hamsters, gerbils and guinea pigs. Paws 'n Hand also handles various home services, making your home appear occupied while you are away, i.e., bringing in mail/newspapers, alternating curtains and/or blinds, taking bins weekly to curbside and back. Choose the schedule and services you prefer, and we'll do the rest!

We would love to welcome you to the Paws 'n Hand family and look forward to hearing from you!

Paws 'n Hand services areas of Oakland, Montclair (Oakland), Oakland Hills (Oakland) and Piedmont.

Meet Leah

Hi all

Leah Duckworth
Owner

LEAH DUCKWORTH is the Owner of Paws ‘n Hand.  Born and raised in Manitowoc, Wisconsin, caring for animals has been a lifelong passion.  She grew up with a beautiful collie, as well as a host of other pets, including a turtle, fish, rabbits, and gerbils.  As a young adult, Leah lived on a farm commune caring for chickens, rabbits, and a delightful pig.  Currently, Leah is the fortunate pet parent to Kala and Lexi, a.k.a. "the wondercats."

Prior to launching Paws ‘n Hand, Leah worked for years as a medical transcriptionist and then decades as a legal secretary.  She was motivated to reinvent her professional life after the economic collapse in 2008. Wiith the assistance of her own beloved pet sitter, that is exactly what happened.  Leah feels fortunate to have discovered her passion in life and could not be happier doing each day what she enjoys most – providing for the happiness and wellbeing of pets.

LEAH DUCKWORTH and her team have been providing stellar pet sitting and dog walking services since 2009.  As the owner and lead professional, Leah was recipient of the National Association for Professional Women’s 2015 Woman of the Year Award for Excellence.

Meet David

Hi all

David Duckworth
Cat Whisperer

Born in Oakland, raised in Piedmont, DAVID DUCKWORTH is a lifelong Bay Area resident. David was raised in a pet-friendly home with multiple cats and dogs throughout his childhood.

He attended naval training in San Diego, then stationed at the Balboa Naval Hospital, San Diego, where David began his career in respiratory therapy. After returning to the Bay Area/San Francisco to continue his career as a registered respiratory therapist, David always had beloved pets in his home.

After retiring from respiratory therapy, David began a new career at WileyX Protective Eyewear, winning numerous awards for his care and attention to detail, as well as his many years of dedicated, valued service.

He has donated to pet shelters around the world, most notably India, where David feels strongly about how dogs and cats need their voices heard there.

For the past eight years David has found being a pet sitter enjoyable and fulfilling, making certain that pets are happy and healthy in their environs, as if they were his own.

David lives in Oakland with his wife, Leah, owner of Paws 'n Hand, and his two magnificent, furry loves, Kala and Lexi (they call him "the cat whisperer!").
